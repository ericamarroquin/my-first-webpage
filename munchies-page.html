All about Munchie the Cat

Munchie is a precious trash cat, found screaming in the garbage bins next to my home. He is orange and white and big-boned by no fault of his own.

Here are a few things that Munchie likes.

People

Munchie is very friendly and loves humans, but he has his favorites.

  • My stepdad
  • My mom
  • Literally anyone else
  • Me, only when all the above are unavailable and I'm very busy

Foods

A picky cat he is not, but here are a few of his favorites:

  • Pieces of plastic on the floor
  • Strings from my favorite, expensive skeins of yarn
  • Fish flavored things

Munchie is so enthralled that you took the time to read about him!
